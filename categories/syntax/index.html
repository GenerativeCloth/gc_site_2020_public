Generative garment design for circularity utilizes generative algorithms to include lifecycle, material use, and circularity data at the garment design phase, shifting towards a more environmentally conscious fashion industry and radically increasing the adoption of circular design.

Generative textile design tools use transformative algorithms to include data in the design stage optimizing digital textile design for multiple objectives simultaneously.
